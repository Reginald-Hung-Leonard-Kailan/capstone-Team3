Coach's Corner

Body Weight

The body weight line graph allows the user to input their body weight along with the date using an "add" button, and edit or delete the information as needed. Keeping track of body weight over time can be important for monitoring progress and making adjustments to diet and exercise routines, as well as identifying potential health issues.

Body Fat %

The Body Fat % line graph tracks the user's body fat percentage over time. Keeping track of body fat percentage can help individuals monitor changes in their body composition and make informed decisions about their diet and exercise routine.

Bench

The Bench Press line graph would display data points of weight lifted . The user can input data by clicking the add button and edit or delete it as needed. Keeping track of Bench press progress is important to monitor strength gains, track workout patterns, and set realistic goals for continued improvement.

Squat

The squat line graph allows the Coach to enter the date and weight of their clients squat workout, and add, edit or delete this information as needed using the add button. By keeping track of progress over time, Coach's can identify trends and measure their clients improvements, enabling them to set goals and adjust the workouts accordingly for better results. Regularly tracking this information can help clients stay motivated, maintain accountability, and achieve their fitness goals.

Dead Lift

The Dead Lift line graph displays a clients progress in lifting weight over time. The user inputs their date and weight information through the add button, and can edit or delete their records as necessary. Tracking progress is important in order to monitor improvements, identify areas for growth, and establish attainable goals for weight lifting.
